FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need my own Anthropic API key? +

Yes — you bring your own Anthropic API key. This means your conversations are completely private (we never see them), and you only pay for what you use. Most Pro users spend ₹500–₹1500/mo on API costs in addition to the subscription.

Is my data safe? +

Your API key is stored only in your browser's local session — we never receive or store it. Your CRM data and tasks are stored in your account database, encrypted at rest. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Absolutely. Cancel from your billing settings at any time with no penalty. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your data can be exported as CSV before cancellation.

What's the difference between plans? +

All plans include all 4 agents. The main differences are lead limits (50 vs unlimited), AI message quotas, and advanced features like AI lead scoring, auto-draft outreach, white-labeling, and multi-client workspace (Agency only).

Is there a free trial? +

Yes — all paid plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features for your chosen plan tier during the trial period.

Do you support UPI / Indian payment methods? +

Yes. We use Razorpay for billing, which supports UPI, net banking, all major Indian debit/credit cards, and EMI options. INR pricing with GST invoice included.
